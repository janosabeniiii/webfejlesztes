Full-time drivers

  • Max Verstappen:

    Dutch Formula 1 driver for Red Bull Racing, known for his bold and aggressive racing style, which has helped him break numerous records, including becoming the youngest driver to start an F1 race at 17. With multiple World Championships to his name, Verstappen has redefined modern racing with his remarkable skill, strategic instincts, and tenacity on the track. His influence on the sport continues to grow, making him one of F1's most captivating and successful drivers.

  • Sergio Perez:

    Often called "Checo," is a skilled Mexican Formula 1 driver for Red Bull Racing, celebrated for his smooth, tire-conserving style and ability to deliver under pressure. Known for his resilience and consistency, Pérez claimed his first F1 win in 2020 after years of dedication, making him one of the most experienced drivers on the grid. His contributions as a strong teammate and strategic racer have made him an essential part of Red Bull’s success in recent seasons.

  • Lando Norris:

    Talented British Formula 1 driver for McLaren, known for his sharp racing instincts, quick adaptability, and charismatic personality. Since joining F1, he has showcased remarkable consistency and skill, securing multiple podium finishes and emerging as one of the sport's promising young talents. With his competitive spirit and dedication to improvement, Norris has quickly gained a reputation as a future title contender, endearing himself to fans worldwide both on and off the track.

  • Oscar Piastri:

    Australian Formula 1 driver for McLaren, known for his impressive junior career, which saw him win consecutive championships in Formula 3 and Formula 2. With a calm and focused approach, Piastri has adapted quickly to F1, showing great potential with his precise driving and racecraft. His rapid progress and maturity on the track have already positioned him as one of the most exciting young talents in the sport, with fans and experts alike eagerly watching his development.

  • Charles Leclerc:

    Gifted Monegasque Formula 1 driver for Ferrari, renowned for his speed, precision, and determination. Known for his ability to extract maximum performance from his car, Leclerc has achieved multiple pole positions and race wins, solidifying his reputation as a top contender. With a fierce competitive spirit and relentless drive to bring Ferrari back to championship glory, he has become a fan favorite and a central figure in the current generation of F1 talent.

  • Carlos Sainz:

    Spanish Formula 1 driver for Ferrari, admired for his racecraft, consistency, and technical knowledge. Often referred to as one of the most adaptable drivers on the grid, Sainz has proven his talent across multiple teams, culminating in his role as a key player at Ferrari. With his calculated approach and strong teamwork skills, he has achieved podiums and race victories, earning respect as a resilient competitor and a reliable contender in the F1 field.

  • Lewis Hamilton:

    British Formula 1 driver for Mercedes and one of the most successful drivers in the sport’s history, with seven World Championships to his name. Known for his exceptional skill, precision, and relentless drive, Hamilton has broken numerous records, including the most race wins, and has consistently pushed the boundaries of what is possible on the track. Beyond racing, he is also a powerful advocate for diversity and environmental sustainability, making him not only a dominant force in F1 but also an influential figure globally.

  • George Russel:

    British Formula 1 driver for Mercedes, recognized for his precise driving, composure, and competitive spirit. Rising through the ranks as a standout in junior categories, Russell quickly made a name for himself in F1 with impressive performances in less competitive machinery before joining Mercedes. Known for his intelligence on the track and dedication to improvement, he has already secured podiums and is regarded as one of the brightest young stars, with a promising future as a championship contender.

  • Fernando Alonso:

    Spanish Formula 1 driver with two World Championships to his name, widely regarded as one of the most skilled and versatile drivers in the sport. Known for his relentless drive, tactical intelligence, and exceptional racecraft, Alonso has achieved success across various teams, including Renault, Ferrari, and McLaren. After a brief hiatus from F1, he returned with Aston Martin, proving that his passion and competitive edge remain sharp. With over two decades in F1, Alonso continues to be a respected figure, admired for his experience and relentless pursuit of victory.

  • Lance Stroll:

    Canadian Formula 1 driver for Aston Martin, known for his consistency and strong race performances, particularly in wet conditions. Coming from a background in motorsport, Stroll made his F1 debut at a young age and quickly earned recognition for his ability to secure impressive results, including podium finishes. Despite being in a competitive field, he has demonstrated resilience and determination, balancing his strong technical skills with his desire to continue growing and contributing to his team's success.

  • Yuki Tsunoda:

    Japanese Formula 1 driver for Visa CashApp RB (formerly Alphatauri), known for his aggressive driving style, speed, and raw talent. Since his F1 debut in 2021, Tsunoda has shown promise with his ability to adapt quickly and deliver competitive performances, often pushing his car to its limits. Although still developing his consistency, he has gained valuable experience and continues to improve, with a growing reputation as one of the sport's exciting young talents on the grid.

  • Daniel Ricciardo:

    Australian Formula 1 driver known for his charismatic personality, exceptional overtaking skills, and trademark smile. With multiple Grand Prix wins to his name, Ricciardo has established himself as one of the most entertaining and skilled drivers in the sport. After stints with Red Bull Racing, Renault, and McLaren, he joined AlphaTauri in 2023, seeking to regain his competitive edge. Ricciardo’s ability to perform under pressure, combined with his experience and positive attitude, has made him a fan favorite and a respected figure in F1.

  • Nico Hulkenberg:

    German Formula 1 driver known for his consistency, technical ability, and strong performances, despite never securing a podium finish in his long career. With experience across several teams, including Williams, Force India, and Renault, Hülkenberg has earned a reputation as a reliable and talented driver, often outpacing his teammates. After a brief hiatus, he made a return to F1 with Haas in 2023, continuing to showcase his skill and racecraft. His versatility and resilience make him a respected and experienced presence on the grid.

  • Kevin Magnussen:

    Danish Formula 1 driver for Haas, known for his aggressive racing style and strong performances in challenging conditions. Since debuting in F1 in 2014, Magnussen has built a reputation for his fearless driving and ability to extract the most from his car, often securing valuable points for his team. He returned to Haas in 2022 after a brief hiatus and has continued to be a reliable and determined competitor, known for his ability to battle hard on track and provide valuable feedback to his team.

  • Pierre Gasly:

    French Formula 1 driver for Alpine, known for his resilience, speed, and strategic racecraft. After a challenging start with Red Bull Racing, Gasly rebounded with impressive performances at Toro Rosso (now AlphaTauri), including a memorable victory at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix. His move to Alpine in 2023 marked a new chapter, where he continues to demonstrate his skill and consistency. Gasly is respected for his ability to adapt and compete at a high level, making him a key figure in the F1 grid.

  • Esteban Ocon:

    French Formula 1 driver for Alpine, known for his strong racecraft, consistency, and ability to deliver solid results under pressure. After a successful career in the junior categories, Ocon made his F1 debut in 2016 and quickly gained recognition for his competitiveness, particularly in battles with established drivers. Since joining Alpine, Ocon has been a reliable and valuable asset to the team, securing podium finishes and consistently fighting for points. His determination and strategic approach have established him as a respected figure in the sport.

  • Alexander Albon:

    Thai-British Formula 1 driver for Williams, known for his strong racecraft and ability to extract maximum performance from challenging situations. After debuting in F1 with Toro Rosso (now AlphaTauri), Albon was promoted to Red Bull Racing in 2019, where he showed promise but faced tough competition. After a brief period away from the top team, he returned to F1 with Williams in 2022, helping the team improve their performance with consistent finishes and valuable points. Albon is regarded as a talented and adaptable driver with a bright future in the sport.

  • Logan Sargeant:

    American Formula 1 driver for Williams, making his debut in the 2023 season. As the first American to race in F1 in over a decade, Sargeant brings a fresh perspective and excitement to the sport. After a successful junior career, including a podium finish in Formula 2, he made the leap to F1 with Williams, where he continues to develop his skills. Known for his work ethic and determination, Sargeant is focused on establishing himself as a competitive driver while representing the growing interest in motorsport in the United States.

  • Valtteri Bottas:

    Finnish Formula 1 driver for Alfa Romeo, known for his speed, consistency, and professionalism. Bottas gained widespread recognition during his time with Mercedes, where he played a crucial role in supporting the team’s championship successes while securing multiple race wins and podiums. After joining Alfa Romeo in 2022, Bottas brought his experience and technical expertise to the team, helping them develop their car and improve their performance. With a calm and focused demeanor, he remains a respected and competitive presence in F1.

  • Guanyu Zhou:

    Chinese Formula 1 driver for Alfa Romeo, making history as the first Chinese driver to compete in the sport since 2013. After a successful career in Formula 2, where he secured multiple podiums and finished as a championship contender, Zhou made his F1 debut in 2022. Known for his consistency, smooth driving style, and quick adaptation, he has impressed both fans and teammates with his performances. Zhou's presence in F1 has increased interest in motorsport in China, and he continues to develop his skills, aiming for stronger results in the coming seasons.
